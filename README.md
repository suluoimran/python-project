###  SMART WATER MANAGEMENT PLATFORM FOR AFRICAN REAL ESTATE
## PROJECT DISCRIPTION
This is a platform that reduces water waste,improves transparency between landlords and tenants meanwhile suppporting urban water management
This project tend to real life problems,generate revenue and can expand profits to the real estate owners
### A PROBLEM IN THE SOCIETY

In traditional real estate and apartment management, tracking water meters manually often leads to calculation errors, delayed invoicing, and undetected water leaks. 
Even for the tenants living there some of them can't keep record or track of water usage so this leads to getting high water bills

### HOW MY SYSTEM WILL SOLVE THE PROBLEM

This system bridges that gap by allowing landlords or property managers to seamlessly log data for multiple tenants, generate instant financial breakdowns, 
and automatically run high-consumption evaluations to detect irregular usage but not just them also tenants can also keep track of their of water usage at a ceratin period of time

### KEY FEATURES.
1. Multi-User Data Loop: efforlessly accept data for an unlimited number of tenants in a single session.

2. Smart Input Normalization: Automatically standardizes names using title casing (.title()) and meter numbers to uppercase (.upper()) for clean, professional reports.

3. High_usage_evaluation Auditing: Automatically isolates high-risk bills and prints targeted warnings for heavy consumers.

### FUTURE ROADMAP
i. Integration with a database (SQLite/PostgreSQL) to persist tenant records over multiple months.

ii. Automated PDF invoice generation and WhatsApp/Email API integration to send bills directly to tenants.

iii. A web-based dashboard for visual consumption graphs.

iv.  A potential business model: by paying subscription at a certain period of time
